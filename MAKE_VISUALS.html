Sit down, open a sim, record or screenshot, done. All configs below are pre-set.

Recording Tools
Kap — free, Mac, exports GIF natively. Best choice.
QuickTime → export as video → convert to GIF with ezgif.com
⌘+Shift+5 → record selected area (video, then convert)

Recommended: hide the sidebar in Kap, record just the canvas area. Target ~600–800px wide. Keep GIFs under 5MB.

Quick Tips
Canvas size: Before recording, drag the browser window wider — more canvas = higher-res GIF. Hide bookmarks bar (⌘+Shift+B) to maximize space.
Speed slider: For GIFs, try speed=0.5 to slow the physics down — easier to see the motion and looks more deliberate on screen.
GIF size: Keep under 5MB for web. In Kap, set frame rate to 15fps (not 30) to halve file size with almost no visible quality loss. Or use ezgif.com optimizer after export.
Priority order: #01 (mutiny GIF) is the most important. If you only make one thing, make that.
